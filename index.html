About
Peter Kim is a multidisciplinary designer specializing in visual design and animation. For the past 6 years, Peter has collaborated with a variety of clients from tech startups to university branding departments, where he has applied his unique aesthetic to a variety of mediums and business initiatives.

As a creative director, Peter brings a set of diverse skills and experience to clients, offering more than a typical designer.
